Published in: 		Employment effects of the new German minimum wage (SOEP dataset)

Description: 		'This file is about the estimation and graphical analysis of different bites, namely the Fraction Index and Kaitz Index for each German State each year.
			The Fraction Index is the ratio of affected individuals by the minimum wage, hence all that earn less than 8.50 Euro per hour.
			The Kaitz Index is ration between the minimum legal wage and the average wage'
